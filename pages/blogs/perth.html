Trip to Perth.

Some memories from the trip to Perth.

I took a trip to Perth and UWA in August 2019 and I would like to share the wonderful memories during this trip to you in this blog.

The trip

I followed the school to the University of Western Australia for a study tour, so we lived in the student apartment of trinity college, and the food was also arranged by the school. I heard that there was a popular Italian restaurant called ciao, so it was a pity that we didn't try it. The price of a cheap meal is less than 10 yuan, and there are more than 20-30 in the city center. The main focus is on fish and chips, steak and such.

The first week we went to Whiteman Park to feed the kangaroos, strolled around King's Park and the Swan Valley, and the Little Blue House, (because Manchester United was also in Perth at that time, the Little Blue House was dressed up as the Little Red House)

Our tour itinerary had only one day free arrangement time, we originally wanted to go to the pink lake wave valley train lobster project ...... fantasy are shattered 😓. But finally we choose to go to Rottnest Island, which also a great experience 😄. We used Flying Pig to buy the round trip electronic tickets, on the island and then buy bus tickets. (quokka is actually not 🐭 but short 🦘) The most interesting thing is: when we were dining at noon, a seagull stole half a plate of fries, and kick the plate to the floor, then quokka picked it up and ate it.

The next week we went to London Street and had a long chat with a painting store owner. Touched a gold nugget at the Perth Mint (thief heavy.) Then also visited some of the labs at the University of Western Australia. I also wanted to go to a winery, but I had to give up because it was too far away. Finally, I bought some health products to go back to the old 😂. The main is Swisse, healthy care, Blackmore three. Lanolin is cheap and good, when the hand letter is excellent.

In general, the pace of life here is very slow, and people are quite harmonious with each other. For example, if you can't find your card or you run out of money, the driver won't rush you. When you get off the bus, the locals are used to saying thank you to the driver. I feel everything is very comfortable.
